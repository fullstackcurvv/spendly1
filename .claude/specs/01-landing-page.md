# Spec: Landing Page

## Overview

The Spendly landing page is the public-facing entry point for an expense-tracking web app. Target audience is individuals who want to understand their personal spending habits. The conversion goal is account registration via "Start tracking free" / "Create free account" CTAs, with a secondary goal of driving sign-ins for existing users.
